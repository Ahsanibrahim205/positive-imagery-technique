When can you use the positive mental imagery technique?

  • When you are faced by demands that challenge you, you may
    1. feel pressurised and anticipate failure
    2. think of the dire negative consequences of such failure
    3. exaggerate the negative possibilities of the circumstance.
    4. experience these situations via negative images
  • Using Positive Mental Imagery in advance of an event or even during the event can be useful when:
    1. Giving a presentation, conducting a difficult negotiation or attending an important meeting.
    2. Having an important one-to- one discussion such as a job interview, performance review or media interview.
    3. Coping with or flourishing in a major life event such as getting married taking on a large loan such as a mortgage making a decision to change career or accepting greater responsibility at work, within the family, or socially.
