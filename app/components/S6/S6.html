Contingency planning imagery

  • By building into your rehearsal additional mental images of you experiencing one or more performance problems and how you will successfully manage them. Examples could be to decide what would you do if:
    1. during an interview you are asked a question which you have not anticipated and you find difficult to immediately answer
    2. during a negotiation someone asks you for information which you have not prepared
    3. a major customer, who you have contacted about a new order, starts to complain unexpectedly about the performance of your product
  • Anticipate and experience in your mind’s eye, imagine:
    1. being in the difficult situation that you are going to be confronted with.
    2. sense and experience the negative emotional impact.
  • ‘Play through in your mind’ the image of you successfully executing the behaviours required to cope with that situation and move past the problem.
  • For example, you might choose to include in your imagery pictures of yourself using other Wellness Techniques such as the quieting reflex and negative thought stopping.
  • Congratulations. You are now using contingency planning imagery.
